about me

hey, i'm jill. i'm a digital designer pursuing a user experience and interaction design degree at drexel university.

thanks for stopping by!

likes 👍

  • sans-serif fonts
  • black coffee
  • shades of green

dislikes 👎

  • slow walkers

originally from new jersey, i'm currently living in philadelphia. i like using design to create enjoyable and memorable experiences, with an emphasis on smooth usability.

my favorite part of ux/ui design is its interdisciplinary nature. my background in fine art allows me to approach visual design with a keen eye and a deep understanding of the principles of design. combined with my passion for problem-solving and programming, i'm able to assess projects with a holistic perspective.

after finishing my work, i like to unwind with a combination of lemon ginger tea, bad tv (i.e. the jersey shore), and good music (my spotify). or maybe good tv and bad music, depending on your point of view!
