sharingexcess.com "about" page redesign

View the live site here.

data-driven decisions

According to Heatmaps, the "About" tab is the most clicked element on the website's home page.

Considering its importance, we needed the page to be updated with relevant information, sectioned into easily readable and navigable sections.

I was tasked with leading this redesign, and I designed this project from concept to deployment.

channeling the brand voice

Sharing Excess uses the wave shape more than any other shape in their visual identity. The wave indicates an approachable flexibility and an easygoing personality that represents the mission of the organization.

the sharing excess warehouse

Inspired by this motif, I wanted to incorporate this organic shape into the website.

I started by creating an SVG image, but the shape was not responsive in the way I intended. To solve this problem, I made different SVG images with different ratios to use at different breakpoints.
